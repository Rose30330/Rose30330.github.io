About

Introduction

LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Poojappura,Trivandrum,starting functiong in 2001,is the first women Engineering college in the state, managed by LBS Center for Science and Technology, the premier institution of Computer training was established by the Government of Kerala in 1976. The center is administered by a governing body and an executive committee.

Vision

To be center of academic exellence empowering women in technical domain

Mission

Imparting value based technical education for transforming young women to professionalsexcelling globally in academics,research&development and industrymeeting societial changes

Gallary

Department

Department of Electronics and Communication

Department of ECE started in year 2001,offers B.Tech in Electronics and communication &Engineering(ECE) and Applied Electronics and Instrumentation(AEI) since 2001 and M.Tech in signal Processing from 2012 onwards.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering started in the year 2001.offeres two batches of B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering(CSE). CSE is highly technical and demanding discipline that offers rewarding and challenging possibilities for the studenys.

Department of Civil Engineering

Department of Civil Engineering started in the year 2012,offers B.Tech programme in Civil Engineering(CE) with an annual intake of 60 students.

Department of Information Technology

The Department offers one B.Tech in Information Technology(IT). IT and CSE are the disciplines within the same realm of study.Information Technology is an academic discipline mostly dealing with industry oriented learning.It involves more practical that have more "real world" applications such as database management and networking, data querying and more.The department provides state of the art computing facilities to the students.Graduates from department are most wanted by both academia and corporates.
