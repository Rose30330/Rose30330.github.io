About

Introduction

LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Poojappura,Trivandrum,starting functiong in 2001,is the first women Engineering college in the state, managed by LBS Center for Science and Technology, the premier institution of Computer training was established by the Government of Kerala in 1976. The center is administered by a governing body and an executive committee.

Vision

To be center of academic exellence empowering women in technical domain

Mission

Imparting value based technical education for transforming young women to professionalsexcelling globally in academics,research&development and industrymeeting societial changes
